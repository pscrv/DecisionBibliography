This is how it works.

The application downloads the bibliographic date of decisions of the Boards of Appeal of the European Patent Office, and tries to display them in a friendly way. It also calculates some spurious statistics, that might just, one day, lead to some real information.

The decision texts are not downloaded automatically, but only when someone looks at the decision in question. Thus, the bilbliographic data should be complete, up to the previous month, but the texts will grow only if people are interested in looking.
